# Product Guidelines

## Tone & Voice
- **Friendly & Casual:** All user-facing copy should be approachable, conversational, and encouraging. The app should feel welcoming to agile teams and make planning sessions enjoyable.

## Visual Design & Branding
- **Playful & Colorful:** The interface should feature engaging, vibrant colors and playful animations to keep users engaged and reduce the monotony of estimation meetings.

## Core UX Principles
- **Speed & Low Latency:** The application must be optimized for rapid interaction and real-time updates via WebSockets. Voting and revealing should be instantaneous.
- **Superior Free Solution:** The product aims to be a free and decisively better alternative compared to existing solutions in the market, removing barriers to entry and focusing on core utility without annoying paywalls.
